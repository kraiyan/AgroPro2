Welcome to Balaghat farms

A profession of hope!

History

Due to bad harvest, the producers/farmers find it difficult to build a sustainable life in areas near Balaghat. Farming becomes un-economic forcing farmers to start working as laborers. Resulting in social problems, as disturbance and disruption of economic and personal life.

The bad harvest was caused by the following problems
  • No assured water resources.
  • No plan to tackle constant climate change.
  • Non affordable cost of cultivation of land.

Our Vision

Balaghat Farms’ aims to tackle these problems by:
  • Giving assured help and advice to these farmers.
  • Helping especially marginal land holding farmers having 1-5 acres of land by ensuring a sustainable life and helping them in covering basic needs.Ensuring this by building a community of farmers, through which making agriculture sustainable, which will be climate change resilient.
  • Also from the consumer point, ensuring a clean and green supply chain.
